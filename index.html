Best Selling Books

ALL THE LIGHT WE CANNOT SEE

Author - Anthony Doerr

"All the Light We Cannot See" is a Pulitzer Prize-winning historical fiction novel by Anthony Doerr, set during World War II, that follows the story of a blind French girl and a German boy whose paths eventually cross, resulting in a powerful and emotional tale of survival and human connection.

THE POWER OF YOUR SUBCONSCIOUS MIND

Author - Joseph Murphy

"The Power of Your Subconscious Mind" is a self-help book by Joseph Murphy that explores the power of the subconscious mind and offers practical techniques for harnessing its potential to achieve success, happiness, and personal fulfillment.

THE PSYCHOLOGY OF MONEY

Author - Morgan Housel

"The Psychology of Money" by Morgan Housel is a finance book that examines the psychological aspects of money and how they impact financial decisions. With real-life examples and insights, Housel provides valuable advice on risk management, investing, and financial planning.

THE RETREAT

Author - Sarah Pearse

"The Retreat" by Sarah Pearse is a thrilling mystery novel about a detective investigating a suspicious death at a remote wellness retreat in the Swiss Alps.

ATOMIC HABITS

Author - James Clear

"Atomic Habits" is a bestselling self-help book written by James Clear. The book offers a comprehensive guide to building and maintaining positive habits that can lead to personal and professional success.

Think Like a Monk

Author - Jay Shetty

Think Like a Monk is a self-help book by Jay Shetty that provides practical advice on mindfulness, spirituality, and personal growth, based on his experience as a monk. It offers guidance on finding inner peace, improving relationships, and discovering one's purpose in life.

Our Recommendations

Something I Never Told You

Author - Shravya Bhinder

"Something I Never Told You" is a powerful novel by Shravya Bhinder that delves into the intricate dynamics of an Indian family and the secrets that threaten to unravel them. The story follows Radhika as she searches for her missing husband and uncovers startling truths. It's an emotionally charged family drama that will keep readers engaged until the very end.

THE POWER OF YOUR SUBCONSCIOUS MIND

Author - Joseph Murphy

"The Power of Your Subconscious Mind" is a self-help book by Joseph Murphy that explores the power of the subconscious mind and offers practical techniques for harnessing its potential to achieve success, happiness, and personal fulfillment.

The Woman in the Window

Author - A.J. Finn

"The Woman in the Window" is a thrilling novel by A.J. Finn about an agoraphobic woman who witnesses a violent act and becomes entangled in a dangerous mystery. With unexpected twists and a gripping plot, this psychological thriller is sure to keep you on the edge of your seat until the very end.

THE RETREAT

Author - Sarah Pearse

Chanakya Neeti

Author - Radhakrishnan Pillai

"Chanakya Neeti" by Radhakrishnan Pillai is a book that presents the teachings of Chanakya, an ancient Indian philosopher, on a wide range of topics. It offers practical and timeless advice on politics, economics, ethics, and human behavior, providing readers with a guide to living a successful and fulfilling life. This book is a must-read for anyone interested in Indian philosophy and the art of living.

Think and Grow Rich

Author - Napoleon Hill

"Think and Grow Rich" by Napoleon Hill is a self-help book that offers practical advice for achieving success. Based on interviews with successful people, the book emphasizes the power of positive thinking and visualization, and outlines practical steps for setting and achieving goals. This influential book has inspired millions of readers to pursue their dreams and achieve success in their personal and professional lives, making it a must-read for anyone seeking to improve their life.
